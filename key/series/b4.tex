\noindent
Does $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac1{n^{0.9}}$
diverge, converge absolutely, or converge conditionally?

\begin{itemize}

\item {\bf Solution.} The series $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac1{n^{0.9}}$ diverges by the $p$-test since $p=0.9$.

\end{itemize}
