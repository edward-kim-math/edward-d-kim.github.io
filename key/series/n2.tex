\noindent
Does $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^{2/3}}$
diverge, converge absolutely, or converge conditionally?

\subsection*{Solution}

$\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^{2/3}}$ is a $p$-series with $p=\frac23$. Since $p \leq 1$, the series $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^{2/3}}$ diverges by the $p$-series test.
